CCET(DG): 4 Must-Try Campus Beverages

Rupali
Last Updated: August 22, 2024

1. Italian Soda at Bytes

Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Rediscover the refreshing blend of fizzy water and fruit syrup with Bytes' Italian sodas. Less sweet than regular sodas and more exciting than flavored water, these drinks offer a perfect caffeine-free alternative.

Recommendation: Raspberry Italian Soda

Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

3. Latte at CoHo

Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Discover the delightful Vanilla Bean Latte at CoHo. While it may be the only place offering this particular drink, the taste is sure to impress and keep you coming back for more.

Recommendation: Vanilla Bean Latte

4. Agua Fresca at Nexus Cafe

Near Block A, CCET(DG), Sector 26

Nexus Cafe offers a refreshing escape with its Agua Fresca. Enjoy this fruity beverage in their spacious indoor or outdoor seating areas, perfect for a sunny day break.

Recommendation: Agua Fresca
